--- layout: archive title: "Portfolio" permalink: /portfolio/ author_profile: true --- {% include base_path %} Here you can find my non-academic obsessions. If you ever see me at a conference feel free to approach me to talk linguistic and non-linguistic things too! --- **Contemporary fiction** I could say just books in general, but I do have a particular affection for fiction written by young women. I love novels and short stories alike. I am currently obsessed with Camila Sosa Villada (Arg.) and Sally Rooney (Irl.). You can find my current reads on my [Goodreads](www.goodreads.com/dalieus). --- **Cultural journalism** In a past life I was a cultural journalist and a dancer working in a newspaper. I loved getting to read new book releases, watching performances three times a weeek and coming home to write about them. Part of my heart still belongs to the performative arts, so I dive every now and then into the scene. --- **Coffee** I might have a tattoo of a coffee cup. While I like talking coffee notes, extraction methods and brewing techniques, coffee to me is more tied to my friends and family. It has always been my way of connecting with people. --- **Food and baking** I'm Mexican, I take the quality of my tortillas seriously. Living abroad has been a challenge in this regard but it pushed me to cook more and find a deep pleasure in achieving flavours that my grandma would be proud of.
