Here you can find my non-academic obsessions. If you ever see me at a conference feel free to approach me to talk linguistic and non-linguistic things too!

Contemporary fiction

I could say just books in general, but I do have a particular affection for fiction written by young women. I love novels and short stories alike. I am currently obsessed with Camila Sosa Villada (Arg.) and Sally Rooney (Irl.). You can find my current reads on my Goodreads.

Cultural journalism

In a past life I was a cultural journalist and a dancer working in a newspaper. I loved getting to read new book releases, watching performances three times a week and coming home to write about them. Part of my heart still belongs to the performative arts, so I dive every now and then into the scene.

Mexico!

Mexican books, mexican food, mexican music, mexican dances. I have had the inmense honor of visiting 23 of the 32 states in Mexico. Mexico City is, aside from my hometown, my happy place. I will always be ready to rant about how amazing that place is.

Coffee

I might have a tattoo of a coffee cup. While I like talking coffee notes, extraction methods and brewing techniques, coffee to me is more tied to my friends and family. It has been my way of connecting with people.
